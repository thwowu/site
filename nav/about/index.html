About

About me

I'm a data expert on the entire data lifecycle, from designing and maintaining robust ETL/ELT pipelines to developing insightful reports and dashboards. I'm proficient in SQL, Python, and a variety of data engineering tools (AWS, Snowflake, dbt, Fivetran, etc.), and I'm always eager to learn and adopt new technologies.

I've had the opportunity to work across diverse sectors, including education technology, creative industry, real-estate, and fintech, where I've seen firsthand the transformative power of data-driven insights.

What Drives Me

I'm passionate about using data to solve real-world problems and create positive change. Whether it's optimizing business processes, uncovering hidden patterns, or ensuring data privacy, I find fulfillment in knowing that my work has a tangible impact.

I believe that effective data analysis is not just about numbers, but about finding the narrative within the data and communicating it in a way that resonates with stakeholders and inspires action.

My Approach

I take a collaborative and results-oriented approach to every project. I believe in building strong relationships with colleagues, stakeholders and partners, understanding their unique needs, and tailoring my solutions to their specific goals. I'm not afraid to roll up my sleeves and dive into the details, but I also have a keen eye for the bigger picture and can translate complex findings into clear, actionable recommendations.
